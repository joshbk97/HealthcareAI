    From Data to Diagnosis - Empowering Doctors, Assuring Patients

    Welcome to MADI - Medical Analysis & Diagnostics Interface.

    Madi is a cutting-edge, ai-driven web application designed to assist medical professionals with precision diagnostics across multiple medical domains. Using state-of-the-art machine learning models trained on specific body parts—such as the liver, kidneys, heart, and lungs, madi can accurately detect a wide range of diseases based on patient scans and data input.

    Each model provides a detailed analysis, complete with a percentage-based prediction of the likelihood of various health conditions. These results are seamlessly aggregated into a comprehensive report, making it easier for doctors to analyze and cross-reference multiple diagnoses.

    Madi’s integrated chatbot helps physicians consolidate complex medical data, identifying the most probable diagnosis for each patient. Additionally, the app generates a patient-friendly summary, translating medical jargon into easy-to-understand language, ensuring clarity for patients and their families.

Key Features


Multi-modal Machine Learning Models

Specialized models for different organs and conditions (e.g., kidney analysis, liver disease prediction, etc.).

AI Aggregation

All results are compiled into a unified analysis, aiding doctors in making confident decisions.

ChatBot Assistance

AI-guided summaries for diagnosing complex cases based on aggregated data.

Patient-friendly Summaries

Auto-generated reports explained in simple, non-technical terms for patient communication.
